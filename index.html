Economy

Tokenomics

DogeBlack AI's tokenomics, with a grand total of 5 billion tokens, is smartly constructed to fuel our ecosystem. The Marketing and Development Wallets hold 10% and 1% respectively, driving branding and technical advancements.


A substantial 25% is allocated for CEX listings, endorsing a varied trading platform for users. Presale holds 30%, boosting community engagement and initial fundraising. To ensure fluid transactions, 10% is dedicated to PancakeSwap liquidity.


We've designated 14% for regular burns to maintain token value and control inflation. Overall, DogeBlack AI's tokenomics signifies our dedication to a thriving, balanced, and profitable ecosystem.


Our token will have a fee of 3% for purchases and 5% for sales, Please note that unsold tokens will be burned.
